About

Born on the island of Hawaii but raised on the island of Oahu, I grew up knowing only what the islands had to offer (Which is not that bad, condsidering paradise and all...). Not long after graduating from Kailua High School I moved to Las Vegas, NV. An opportunity presented itself to leave the islands, so I took it and never looked back. While in Las Vegas I held a few jobs but most of my adult career was spent working my way up the banking industry ladder. Tired of the fast life that Las Vegas provided, my wife and I decided to relocate to Richland, WA to slow down, establish new careers, and start a family of our own. Tired of working in a customer service position (Not exactly fitting for my personality), my wife and I have decided that it is a good time for me to pursue a Bachelors Degree in hopes of gaining a career that is more suited for my personality.
